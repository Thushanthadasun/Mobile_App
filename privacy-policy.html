Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15 Aug 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Silva Motor Stores (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our mobile app and related services.

1. Information we collect

  • Account info: name, email, phone number.
  • Vehicle & booking info: vehicle details, photos (if you upload), appointment time, service history.
  • Payment info: handled by our payment gateway PayHere. We do not store full card details on our servers.
  • Technical data: device info, app version, IP, logs, cookies/identifiers as applicable.

2. How we use your information

  • Provide, schedule, and manage services.
  • Process payments and send invoices/receipts.
  • Customer support, safety, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.
  • Improve our app, analytics, and communications (with your consent where required).

3. Sharing of information

  • Service providers: hosting, analytics, notifications, payment processing (PayHere).

4. Payments

We use PayHere to process payments. Your card details are submitted directly to PayHere over secure connections and are not stored by us. PayHere’s use of your data is governed by their privacy policy.

5. Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, to meet legal/accounting requirements, or to resolve disputes.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data. Contact us using the details below.

8. Children

Data may be processed on servers outside your country. We take steps to protect your information consistent with this policy.
