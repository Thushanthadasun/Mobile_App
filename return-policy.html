Return & Refund Policy

Effective Date: 15 Aug 2025

Thank you for using Silva Motor Stores (“we”, “our”, “us”). This policy explains how returns, cancellations, and refunds work for services and products purchased through our mobile application.

1. Scope

This policy applies to service bookings (e.g., inspections, repairs, maintenance) and any related parts/products purchased through the Silva Motor Stores app.

2. Cancellations & Rescheduling (Services)

  • Free reschedule/cancel up to 24 hours before the scheduled time.
  • Late cancellation (within 24 hours): a fee up to 25% of the booking value may apply to cover technician/time allocation.
  • No‑show: considered service rendered; no refund.

3. Refunds (Services)

  • If we cannot deliver the service due to our fault, you may choose a full refund or a free reschedule.
  • Quality concerns will be investigated within 5 business days. If approved, we will provide a rework or partial/full refund based on findings.

4. Products/Parts

  • Unopened/unused parts can be returned within 7 days of delivery with original packaging and proof of purchase.
  • Installed/used electrical or consumable parts are not eligible for return unless defective on arrival.
  • Defective items will be repaired/replaced as per manufacturer’s warranty terms.

5. How refunds are issued

Payments made via PayHere will be refunded to the original payment method. Processing times depend on your bank/issuer (typically 5–10 business days after our approval). Service fees charged by payment processors may be non‑refundable where applicable.

6. Proof & inspection

We may require order numbers, photos, videos, or technician inspection to validate a claim.
